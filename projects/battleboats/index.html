B2! You sunk my battleship!

Battleboats is a top-down, real-time, tactically-chaotic artillery game. You are given command of a battleship with a singular mission: sink all ships in the enemy's fleet. Pick from different classes of ships, go from the meagre battleship to destroyers and aircraft carriers. Deck out the deck with a variety of turrets.
